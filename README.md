## Zenith: Bridging Space, Technology, and Geopolitics

Welcome to the official repository for Zenith, a media-driven platform dedicated to exploring the final frontier through the lenses of cutting-edge technology and global strategy. This website serves as the digital hub for our content across Instagram and Medium, providing deep dives into lunar robotics, space startups, and the evolving landscape of international space policy.

## Project Overview

**Zenith** aims to democratize complex space-tech narratives. Whether it's analyzing the Indian space budget or the technical nuances of the Artemis missions, this platform is built for enthusiasts who want to see the bigger picture of how space exploration shapes our world.

### Core Features

* **Interactive Articles:** Long-form analysis on space-tech and geopolitics.
* **Mission Trackers:** Real-time updates on upcoming lunar and deep-space missions.
* **Multimedia Integration:** Seamless embedding of our Instagram visuals and documentary-style video content.
* **Tech Spotlights:** Dedicated sections for robotics (C++, Arduino, ESP32) and computer vision in space applications.
